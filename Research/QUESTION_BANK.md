# Question Bank

## Soul

- What is the self?
- If I observe the body, can the body be me?
- If I observe thought, can thought be me?
- What remains when body, thought, memory, and emotion are set aside?
- Is *anubhava* the only intrinsic characteristic of the soul?
- What exactly belongs to the soul?
- What does *svarupa* actually mean?
- If every soul is an experiencer, what distinguishes one soul from another?

## Happiness

- Where does happiness exist?
- Is happiness in the object?
- Is happiness in the mind?
- Is happiness intrinsic to the soul?
- If happiness is intrinsic, why is it not experienced continuously?
- If bliss is not intrinsic, what exactly is experienced in liberation?
- Does happiness require knowledge?

## Suffering

- Is suffering merely the absence of happiness?
- Is darkness merely the absence of light?
- Can suffering possess positive reality?
- Can an eternal soul experience something contrary to its nature?
- If suffering is outside the soul, who experiences it?
- If suffering is inside the soul, how is it not intrinsic?

## Beginninglessness

- What does beginningless actually explain?
- Does *anadi* explain existence or only deny temporal origin?
- Can something be beginningless but not eternal?
- Can a beginningless association fail to be essential?
- What exactly is removed when beginningless karma ends?
- Is *pravahanadi* the correct term?

## God

- If only God is independent, what does independence mean?
- Can there be two completely independent realities?
- Can an infinite being completely know an infinite nature?
- Does complete knowledge place a boundary around infinity?
- If God already knows every choice, am I free?
- If God can grant liberation, why not liberate every soul?

## Structural Analogies

- Can potential energy becoming manifest illuminate beginningless karma becoming manifest?
- Where does the physics analogy help?
- Where does it fail?
- Is the analogy about substance or structure?

## Conversation With Tradition

- Which questions require Vyasatirtha?
- Which questions require Madhusudana?
- Which questions require Jayatirtha?
- Which questions have Western parallels?
