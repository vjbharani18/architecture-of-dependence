# Part X: Conversation With Tradition

Volume I remains the enquiry.

This part opens the door to Volume II.

The purpose is not to decorate the manuscript with famous names.

The purpose is to ask:

> Who, in the history of philosophy, asked the closest version of this exact question?

Possible future participants:

- Madhva

- Vyasatirtha

- Aristotle

No thinker should be introduced unless the question genuinely requires them.

## Example

### Can an Eternal Soul Experience Something Contrary to Its Nature?

**Question**

If the soul's essential nature never changes, how can it genuinely experience suffering?

**Traditional Dvaita Response**

To be verified from primary sources.

**Question for Advaita**

Does the empirical/ultimate distinction avoid this problem, or relocate it?

**Comparative Philosophy**

Essential properties versus accidental properties may be relevant.

**Notebook**

The question remains open until experience, property, and relation are distinguished more carefully.
