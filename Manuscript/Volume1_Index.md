# Editorial Principle

This manuscript is organised around **questions**, not doctrines.

No chapter exists merely to explain a philosophical system.

Each chapter records a genuine question that arose during the enquiry.

Traditions are introduced only when the question itself requires them.

The purpose of Volume I is not to arrive at final conclusions.

It is to arrive honestly at the questions that deserve a conversation with tradition.
